Deepsite Prompt — Metro Quick-Start (Dark Theme)

Project: Metro Quick-Start Guides — Dark Landing Page

Goal: One ultra-simple, fast landing page with large, tappable PDF buttons for five guides: Metro Soft, Metro Duo, Metro AIO, Metro 5, and Chlor-Out.

Branding & Theme

Theme: Dark mode only. Background: pure black. Text: near-white.

Palette: Extract gold directly from the uploaded Metro Man logo. Create CSS variables:

--metro-black: #000000

--metro-gold: <extract from logo> (fallback #b79a3b if extraction unavailable)

--text: #f5f5f5

--muted: #bdbdbd

Logo: Use metro_mascot_only.png (Metro Man) in the hero.

Wordmark: Render “METRO” in Franklin Gothic Heavy Italic.

Font

Upload the Franklin Gothic Heavy Italic font file(s) (WOFF2 preferred) and register:

@font-face {
  font-family: 'FranklinGothicHeavyItalic';
  src: url('/assets/fonts/FranklinGothic-HeavyItalic.woff2') format('woff2');
  font-weight: 900;
  font-style: italic;
  font-display: swap;
}


Use: The header brand lockup uses 'FranklinGothicHeavyItalic', Impact, 'Arial Narrow Bold', Arial, sans-serif.

Body font: Inter or system UI stack for readability.

Layout

Header (sticky): Left = METRO wordmark (using the font above), Right = single nav item “Contact”.

Header background: --metro-black; 1px bottom border in #1f1f1f.

Hero Section (centered, compact):

Metro Man logo on the left; headline + subhead on the right (stacked on mobile).

Headline: “Quick-Start Guides” in Franklin Gothic Heavy Italic (italic, gold accent underline).

Subhead: “Easy installation PDFs for our most popular units.” (muted text).

Guides Grid: 2-column desktop, 1-column mobile.

Each card: rounded-xl, soft shadow, 1px border #222.

Card background: #0f0f0f.

Title in near-white; tiny tag “PDF” pill in gold/black.

Two buttons per card: View PDF (primary, gold on black) and Download (outline in gold).

Footer: Small, centered; text in muted color with links. Include © Metro Water Systems, phone 888-309-2837, and sales@wbi72.com.

Buttons (states & a11y)

Primary (gold): background var(--metro-gold), text black; hover = darken gold ~10%; focus ring 2px gold with 1px black offset for contrast.

Outline: transparent background, 1.5px border gold; hover = faint gold wash (rgba(gold, .08)).

Buttons full-width on mobile; keyboard-navigable with visible focus.
